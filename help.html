Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using the voice assistant?

Click the microphone button on the Voice Assistant page or Demo page and speak your command clearly.

What voice commands can I use?
  • "Find me [product name]"
  • "Add [product name] to my cart"
  • "Remove [product name] from my cart"
  • "Show me my cart"
  • "Track my order"
  • "Apply discount code"
  • "Where is the nearest store?"
  • "Help"
Can I use voice commands while shopping in-store?

Yes! Use the In-Store Kiosk mode on tablets available at partner stores for voice-enabled assistance.

Is my data safe when using ShopVerseAI?

We prioritize your privacy and secure your data using best practices. No data is shared without your consent.

Voice Command Usage Tips

  • Speak naturally and clearly for best results.
  • Use simple commands like "Add headphones to cart" or "Track order 12345".
  • Pause briefly between commands if you need to speak multiple instructions.
  • Make sure your microphone is enabled and working.
